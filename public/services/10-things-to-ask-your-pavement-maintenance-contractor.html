10 Things To Ask Your Pavement Maintenance Contractor

  1. Do they have liability insurance?

    Elite Finishes has up to 1 million dollars of coverage.

  2. Will they repair cracks properly?

    Elite Finishes uses D.O.T approved hot pour crack sealant.

  3. How do they apply the sealer?

    Elite Finishes applies sealer by hand around all edges and sprays the remaining asphalt.

  4. Do they add at least 3-5lbs of sand per gallon of sealer?

    Elite Finishes adds 40 bags @ 50lbs each in 500 gallons.

  5. Is their sealer mixed constantly using hydraulic agitation?

    Elite Finishes provides constant hydraulic agitation.

  6. What percentage of water do they add to their mix?

    Elite Finishes adds 33% water to concentrate.

  7. Do they properly neutralize oil spots and stains?

    Elite Finishes uses a neutralizing agent called "Petro Seal".

  8. Do they include latex additives in the mix?

    Elite Finishes uses additives such as Top Tuff and Sealer VM.

  9. Do they warranty their work?

    Elite Finishes offers an untouchable 2-year warranty.

  10. Do they have a 100% satisfaction guaranteed policy?

    At Elite Finishes, if you are not satisfied, it's FREE.
