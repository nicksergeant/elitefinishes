Catch Basin Repair

Over time blocks and bricks used to construct your catch basins begin to fail and deteriorate. This is mainly due to erosion. If the surrounding asphalt around your catch basin is cracked or heaved, it may be time to get them repaired. In order to prevent a costly repair, get the problem fixed as soon as you see signs of failure. Once the blocks and bricks are deteriorated, the main structure is no longer secured. Cars driving over them, cars parking on them and snowplows hitting them create more stress on the steel grates. This makes the entire top structure sink, creating more liability.

Elite Finishes will remove and replace all deteriorated blocks, bricks and mortar, re-level the existing grate and replace the failed surrounding asphalt, giving you a structurally sound catch basin.
